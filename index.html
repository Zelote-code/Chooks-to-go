About Us

Our Vision

To be the most loved roasted chicken brand in every Filipino home, delivering high-quality, flavorful, and affordable meals that bring families together while upholding values of excellence, sustainability, and community care.

Our Mission

To bring joy to every meal through fresh, flavorful, and convenient roasted chicken options for families and individuals alike.

Our Story

Chooks ToGo started as a small local shop with a big dream—to make delicious, high-quality roasted chicken available to everyone. Today, we have grown but our dedication to flavor and quality remains the same.
